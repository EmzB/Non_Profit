About Us

We are Humans First and we offer..

The Humans Fisrt Organization came into being after a group of community members in the the Washington District area decided to take action against the poverty and disease ridden down town area. Perry Pagna named the organization Humans First Org. and the first members consisted of ten individuals. We have since offered shelter, clothing, food and medication from the elderly all the way down to small toddlers..

Our services

Food and Water Donations

Water and Food are basic needs and the funds we acquire are used to purchase these utilities.

Clothing and Community Shelter

We donate clothing given to us by charitable people and offer community shelters to remove people from the streets.

Medical Emergency Support

Our sponsors have helped contribute 2 ambulances that function 24 hours a day to ensure the underpriviledged are treated.
